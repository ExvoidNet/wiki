Founded in May 2021 by Mathias Pepin-Leduc and Mathis Boulanger, Exvoid Network is a Canadian french-based community open and friendly to all multiplayer games enthusiasts, all of our game server(s) are self-hosted locally on our own dedicated hardware.

Minecraft is currently our first game with many more to come in the future!

## Void

Void is our dedicated hosting machine, it is a DELL PowerEdge R710 that is the brain of Exvoid Network.
